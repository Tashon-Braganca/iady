Since you want this to be perfect for her phone/tablet, a custom Heart QR Code is the perfect touch.

Here are the best free tools to generate one:

### Option 1: Unitag (Highly Recommended for Design)
1.  Go to **[unitag.io/qrcode](https://www.unitag.io/qrcode)**
2.  Enter your website URL.
3.  Go to the **"Templates"** tab and look for "Love" or "Heart" styles.
4.  Or go to **"Customization" -> "Eyes"** (the corner squares) and change their color to Pink/Red.
5.  Upload a logo (middle icon) -> Upload a small Heart icon or "I ‚ù§Ô∏è U".

### Option 2: Me-QR (Easiest "Heart Shape")
1.  Go to **[me-qr.com](https://me-qr.com)**
2.  Select "Link / URL".
3.  Paste your Vercel/Netlify link.
4.  Click "Customize & Download QR".
5.  **Frame Category:** Select the "Heart" frame. It wraps the QR code in a cute heart outline.

### Option 3: AI Art QR (The "Wow" Factor)
If you want something that looks like *art* (e.g., a QR code blended into a painting of roses), use:
*   **[HuggingFace QR Code AI](https://huggingface.co/spaces/huggingface-projects/QR-code-AI-art-generator)**
*   **Prompt:** "A beautiful heart shape made of red roses and soft pink ribbons, high quality, 4k"
*   **Content:** Your URL.
*   **Control Strength:** 1.2 (Start here and adjust).
